Retention Strategy Playbook

1. Identify High-Risk Customers

  • Login to RetainX Dashboard and go to Analytics section
  • Filter customers with churn probability > 70%
  • Export the list of high-risk customers
  • Segment them by age group, plan type, and satisfaction score
💡 Pro Tip: Focus on customers with both high churn probability AND high lifetime value first.

2. Deploy Personalized Offers

  • For Price-Sensitive customers: Offer 20-30% discount on next billing cycle
  • For Content-Dissatisfied users: Send personalized content recommendations
  • For Infrequent users: "We miss you" campaign with exclusive preview
  • For Students: Verify student ID and offer semester-based pricing
💡 Pro Tip: Use A/B testing to find which offer works best for each segment.

3. Execute Communication Campaigns

  • Week 1: Send personalized email/SMS to high-risk customers
  • Week 2: Follow up with satisfaction survey
  • Week 3: Share success stories and new features
  • Week 4: Offer loyalty rewards for continued subscription
💡 Pro Tip: Use different communication channels based on customer preference.

4. Track & Measure Results

  • Monitor churn rate weekly using Dashboard analytics
  • Track which offers have highest redemption rate
  • Calculate ROI using the ROI Calculator in Retention page
  • Review customer feedback and satisfaction scores
💡 Pro Tip: Aim for at least 40% retention rate among targeted high-risk customers.

5. Optimize & Repeat

  • Analyze which strategies performed best
  • Update customer segments based on new behavior data
  • Schedule monthly retention campaigns
  • Continuously monitor and adjust offers based on performance
💡 Pro Tip: Run retention campaigns monthly, not just when churn risk is high.

FAQ

  • Q: How often should I run retention campaigns?
    A: Monthly for high-risk customers, quarterly for all customers.
  • Q: What discount works best?
    A: 25% off next month shows best conversion rate (avg 35% redemption).
  • Q: How to measure success?
    A: Track churn rate reduction and campaign ROI using the calculator.
  • Q: What if customer still churns?
    A: Send exit survey to understand reason and improve future strategies.
