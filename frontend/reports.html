Understanding Churn Analysis

What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn occurs when subscribers stop using a service. RetainX predicts churn using Logistic Regression, helping businesses take proactive retention actions.

Logistic Regression

A statistical model that predicts binary outcomes (churn vs no-churn). It calculates probability between 0 and 1 using the sigmoid function.

Risk Categories

High Risk (>70%) - Immediate intervention
Medium Risk (40-70%) - Monitor closely
Low Risk (<40%) - Regular engagement

Key Features

Age, Subscription Plan, NPS Score, Content Satisfaction, Usage Frequency, Monthly Spend, Switching Behavior, Account Sharing

Logistic Regression Formula

P(Churn) = 1 / (1 + e-(β₀ + β₁X₁ + β₂X₂ + ... + βₙXₙ))

Where:
• P(Churn) = Probability of customer churning (0 to 1)
• β₀ = Intercept (base risk)
• β₁...βₙ = Coefficients for each feature
• X₁...Xₙ = Feature values (NPS, Satisfaction, etc.)
